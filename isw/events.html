International Student Welcome Events

Welcome to Cambridge! Each year we organise a number of welcome events to give you a chance to meet old and new students, both post-graduate and undergraduate. All new students are warmly invited to any or all of the events below!

To help us estimate numbers at the two international dinners, if you would like to come please let us know via the website, or email cgs.isw[at]gmail.com. The CICCU dinner is open to all new international students and the dinner on the 10th is aimed mainly at new postgraduate international students. You can still just turn up on the day, but it would be helpful if we have an idea of how many people to expect.

There will also be a give-away of free “Welcome Bags” to new post-graduate students, provided by Friends International & CGS. Each bag contains basic items such as a mug, plate and cutlery to help you survive your first days in Cambridge. Details about when and how to collect your bag will be added here when they are finalised.
