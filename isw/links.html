Links

Some links you may find useful for discovering more about Cambridge:

Cambridge University University homepage.
Cambridge Evening News On-line Cambridge newspaper with local news, events, weather and advertisements.
National Rail Trains in the UK
National Express Coaches Bus timetable and fares for travel around the UK.
Guide to Living in UK Useful information about living in the United Kingdom.
University bulletin board (newsgroups) For accommodation, second hand items, announcements etc 
The Highway Code What you need to know about being a cyclist, pedestrian or driver.
Cambridge University Student Union Support, welfare and campaigns for students
Cambridge University Graduate Union The same but for graduates. Also have a handy shop for all sorts of student needs, and an online forum to ask all sort of questions about life in Cambridge.
cam.misc The Internet discussion group (newsgroup) for Cambridge city and the surrounding area. Inhabited mostly by non-students. See also the frequenty asked questions database for more useful things to know about Cambridge city.
Cambridge Linkline Listening and information support service run by students of the university.
University Counselling Service Professional counselling and advice run by the University.
Christian Graduate Society The Christian Graduate Society (CGS) is a University-recognised society for graduate students which is open to members of all Christian denominations (or of none!). In addition to the International Student Welcome (ISW) programme, we organise a range of social events, Bible discussion groups and talks on Christian themes throughout the year.
Cambridge Inter-Collegiate Christian Union The Cambridge Inter-Collegiate Christian Union (CICCU) exists to make Jesus Christ known to students in Cambridge. CICCU runs a programme of talks throughout the year about Christianity and has college group and University-wide meetings on a weekly basis during term.
