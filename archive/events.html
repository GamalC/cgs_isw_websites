Lent 2010 Events

Saturday 6th February: CGS International Food Evening

7:30pm @ the Henry Martin Hall ([map]), Market St, opposite WHSmith. Invite your Christian and non-Christian friends to enjoy food from around the world. Please do bring a dish from your country to share, and during the evening, there will also be a Gospel talk by Michael Gladwyn.

Contact Matthew Kuo for further details (myhk2 [at] cam.ac.uk)

Tuesday 9th & Thursday 11th February: Rescued? and Pondering over Pudding

7:50pm @ front of King's College. We'll be meeting out the front of King's and walk over to Great St Mary's for the talk at 8pm. Pondering over Pudding will be held at the GU following the talk: an opportunity to discuss the talk with others and share in some great pudding.

Contact Matthew Kuo for further details (myhk2 [at] cam.ac.uk)
