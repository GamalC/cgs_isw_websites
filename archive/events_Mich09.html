Michaelmas 2009 Events

Monday 5th October: CGS Prayer and Praise Evening

7:30pm @ The Round Church ([map]). Please join us in praising God for what He has done and continues to do through CGS as we commit this term's events to Him in prayer.

Contact June Jung for further details (sjj37 [at] cam.ac.uk)

Saturday 10th, 24th October and 21st November: Prayer Breakfasts

9:30am @ Flat 2, 10 St John's Road ([map]).

Contact June Jung for further details (sjj37 [at] cam.ac.uk)

Monday 12th October: CGS Squash

7:30pm @ The Munby Room, King's College ([map]). Want to find out more about CGS; what we do and how you can be involved? Then come along to the CGS Squash to meet some of the CGSers and to drink some squash!

Contact Matthew Kuo for further details (myhk2 [at] cam.ac.uk)

Saturday 7th November: Prayer Lunch

12:30pm @ TBC

Contact June Jung for further details (sjj37 [at] cam.ac.uk)

Saturday 31st October: International Food Evening

7:30pm @ the Henry Martin Hall ([map]), Market St, opposite WHSmith. Come and enjoy food from around the world, and please do bring a dish from your country to share. During the evening, there will also be a short talk by Bartow Wiley.

Contact Eric Appel for further details (ericandrewappel [at] gmail.com)

Saturday 28th November: Christmas Party

7:30pm @ Payne's home (Details to be updated closer to the date). Join us and the ISW crew for a 'traditional' British Christmas Party with mulled fruit punch and fantastic food. A short talk on the meaning of Christmas will also be given.

Contact June Jung for further details (sjj37 [at] cam.ac.uk)

Friday 4th to Sunday 6th December: CGS Weekend Away

Ringsfield Hall (Details to be updated closer to the date). Finish off the year by relaxing with friends at beautiful Ringsfield Hall. Whether it be going for walks, digging into the Bible or even having a dip in the ocean, this is the opportunity to do it away from the stresses of Cambridge.

Contact Michelle Sanders for further details (michelle.l.sanders [at] gmail.com)
