EVENTS FOR EASTER TERM, 2008

      7.30pm @ Henry Martyn Hall (opposite WH Smith) [map for Market Street]
      Bring along some food from your home country to share and sample food from around the world! There will also be a short talk on who Jesus is and his relevance to our lives. Contact Jessica Lam (wl246) and Humberto Gumeta (hag22) for further details.
    • Sat 3rd May : DAY OF PRAYER
      7.30pm @ Location to be announced. Contact Nicole Chan (nmmc2) for details.
    • Sat 17th May : Formal Hall at Selwyn College
      Dress up and enjoy a scrumptious dinner in another college. Contact Ethan Sanders (es431) for further details and to book.
    • 20th-22nd June: External event: National Conference for Christian Postgraduates and Postdoctoral Researchers
      Dovedale House, Derbyshire
      Cost: £65 full board (£10 discount for bookings before 30th April; bookings close on 1st June). Some reduced-fee places are available for those in financial hardship. Contact Remi Tobler (remi.tobler [at-symbol] friendsinternational.org.uk) (This is not a CGS organised event)
