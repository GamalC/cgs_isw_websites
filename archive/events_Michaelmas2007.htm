<EVENTS FOR MICHAELMAS TERM, 2007>

  • Mon 1st Oct, 2007: Day of Prayer
    7.30pm @ 11 Park Terrace [map] (basement flat, buzzer labelled 'Thompsons')
    Join us to pray for the Cambridge graduate community and the world. Contact Angela Symington (ams85 [at] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Sat 6th Oct 2007: International Student Welcome Party
    7.30-10.30pm @ 236 Hills Road, between Homerton College and Addenbrookes [map]. Take bus Citi 1 or Citi 7 - get off at Homerton College - or meet outside the University Arms Hotel at 7pm for a free minibus to the party. Or about 40 mins walk/15 mins cycle from the City Centre. Contact Angela Chew (caamc2 [at] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Mon 8th Oct, 2007: CGS Open Evening (Squash)
    7.30pm @ Senior Parlour, Gonville and Caius College [map]. Interested in joining CGS? Come along to the CGS squash to be part of a vibrant Christian postgrad community.
    Contact Xinyi Su (xs204 [at] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Sat 27th Oct 2007: An Evening with International Food
    7.30pm @ Henry Martyn Hall, Market St, opposite Borders bookshop [map for Market Street]
    Bring along some food from your home country to share and sample food from around the world! There will also be a short talk.
    Contact Jessica Lam (wl246 [at] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Mon 5th Nov 2007: Grad Forum
    7.30pm @ Latimer Room, Clare College [map].
    A thought provoking and challenging talk on the temptations and challenges of being a Christian postgrad. Chris Watkin will be speaking on 'Every Thought Captive: A Christian Perspective in the Arts and Humanities'. Chris is a Junior Research Fellow at Magdalene College.
    Please contact Todd Thompson (tmt25 [at] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Fri 9th Nov 2007: Formal Hall at New Hall
    Don your evening wear and enjoy a scrumptious dinner in another college for only £7 [map]
    Please contact Angela Symington (ams85 [at] cam.ac.uk) to reserve a place.
  • Sat 24th Nov 2007: CGS Christmas Party
    7.30pm @ 236 Hills Road, between Homerton College and Addenbrookes [map].
    Enjoy delicious food and Christmas spirit in a Cambridge family home. There will also be a short talk. Take bus Citi 1 or Citi 7 - get off at Homerton College - or meet outside the University Arms Hotel at 7pm for a free minibus to the party. Or about 40 mins walk/15 mins cycle from the City Centre. Contact Xinyi Su (xs204 [at] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Fri 30th Nov-Sun 2nd Dec: CGS Weekend away at Ringsfield Hall, Suffolk
    Leaving Friday afternoon and returning Sunday late afternoon.
    Explore the Bible and relax with other CGSers in the beautiful East Anglian Countryside
    Contact Anna Thompson (annakay25 [at] gmail.com) for details.
