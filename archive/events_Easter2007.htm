<EVENTS FOR EASTER TERM, 2007>

    • Fri 4th May : DAY OF PRAYER
      7.00pm @ Christ's College, Lloyds Room[map]
      Contact Angela Symington ams85@cam.ac.uk for more details.
    • Sat 5th May: International Food Evening
      7.30pm @ Henry Martyn Hall (opposite WH Smith) [map for Market Street]
      Bring along some food from your home country to share and sample food from around the world! There will also be a short talk. Contact Jessica Lam wl246@cam.ac.uk for more details.
    • 10th-14th May: Chill-out week
      Take a well-earned (hopefully!) rest from the lab or the library to relax will fellow members of your CGS Bible study group. Go on an outing, have a barbecue, go rowing on the Cam. The possibilities are endless. Contact Edward McNeilly ejm61@cam.ac.uk for more information and ideas.
    • Sun 10th June: Grad Forum
      1pm, Link House Garden Room, 7 East Hertford St [map].
      The speaker will be Denis Alexander, director of the Faraday Institute for Science and Religion. Denis is an expert on the interface between science and Christianity, so this promises to be a fascinating talk.
    • Sun 17th June: Garden Party
      3pm-6pm @ Darwin Island, Darwin College [map]
      Including a short talk.
      Contact Anna Thompson akt29@cam.ac.uk for further details.
    • 22nd-24th June: Christian Postgraduate Conference - Excellence in Christ? Following Christ as a postgrad.
      Dovedale House, Derbyshire
      Speakers: Dr Vinoth Ramachandra, Nicole Maturen, Chris Watkin.
      For more info see http://www.christianpostgrad.org.uk (This is not a CGS organised event)
