Our Mission

The Christian Graduate Society (CGS) exists to make Jesus Christ known to the graduate community in Cambridge. The membership and programme reflect the high proportion of international graduates studying at the universities.

Bible Study Groups

The CGS runs three weekly Bible study groups which currently meet on Tuesday and Thursday evenings. These are graduate groups intended to be appropriate both for Christians and for those investigating the Christian faith. Please do invite your friends!

Please contact John Lister (lister [at] dampt.cam.ac.uk) if you would like to join one.
