EVENTS FOR MICHAELMAS TERM 2008

  • Mon 6th Oct: Day of Prayer
    Evening meeting 7.30pm @ Eden Baptist Church [map].
    Join us to pray for the Cambridge graduate community and the world. Contact Sung June Jung (sjj37 [at-symbol] cam.ac.uk) for more details.

    Bring along some food from your home country to share and sample food from around the world! There will also be a short talk.
    Contact Matthew Kuo (myhk2 [at-symbol] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Fri 7th Nov: CGS Café Evening
    7.30pm @ Graduate Union lounge [map].
    Please contact David Sandifer (djs212 [at-symbol] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Sat 29th Nov: CGS Christmas Party
    7.30pm @ 236 Hills Road, between Homerton College and Addenbrookes [map].
    Enjoy delicious food and Christmas spirit in a Cambridge family home. There will also be a short talk. Take bus Citi 1 or Citi 7 - get off at Homerton College - or meet outside the University Arms Hotel at 7pm for a free minibus to the party. Or about 40 mins walk/15 mins cycle from the City Centre. Contact Humberto Gumeta (hag22 [at-symbol] cam.ac.uk) for more details.
  • Fri Fri 5th-Sun 7th Dec: CGS Weekend away at Ringsfield Hall, Suffolk
    Leaving Friday afternoon and returning Sunday late afternoon (transport will be arranged) [map].

    Contact Ruth Hogg (ruthhogg [at-symbol] gmail.com) for details.
