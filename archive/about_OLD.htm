OUR MISSION
Recognising Christ's command to make disciples of all nations, the Christian Graduate Society (CGS) aims to provide opportunities for Christian witness to the graduate student community, and to encourage building-up of Christian graduates. The membership and programme reflect the high proportion of international graduates studying at the University.

BIBLE STUDY GROUPS
The CGS runs five weekly Bible study groups which currently meet on various evenings. These are graduate groups intended to be appropriate for those investigating the Christian faith, and also for Christians. Invite your friends! Please contact John Lister if you would like to join one.

VISA COURSE
We also run a "visa course", which is a discussion group, similar in style to “Alpha”, providing an introduction to the central beliefs of Christianity. Please contact Ard Louis for further details.

WEEKLY PRAYER MEETING
Every week, 4pm on Sundays at Michael Johnstone's house: 21 Lower Park Street. Please contact Michael for details.

CGS SQUASH - OCT 8th, 2001
To be held at the Henry Martyn Hall starting at 7.30pm. Our first meeting of the year - A chance to meet members of the CGS and hear what we do.
