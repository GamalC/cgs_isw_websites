<EVENTS FOR LENT TERM, 2007>

  • Sat 19th Jan 2008: An Evening with International Food
    Henry Martyn Hall, Market St, opposite WHSmith, 7.30pm.
    Bring some food from your home country to share and sample dishes from around the world! There will also be a short talk given by James Pool. Contact Jessica Lam (wl246) for more details.
  • 4th-8th Feb, 2008: CICCU Main Event
    This year's theme is 'Life'. Come and hear Nathan Buttery and Vaughan Roberts in a series of lunchtime and evening talks exploring what the Bible says about how Jesus is the way and the truth and the life. See the Life08 website for more details.
  • 11th-17th Feb 2008: Chill-out week
    During this week we will make it a priority to 'chill' together with our Bible study groups (and invite other friends too!) - a great break during this busy term. More details to follow.
  • 29th Feb-4th Mar 2008: Veritas Forum at Cambridge
    'Personal faith in the public sphere'. Ponder and discuss this controversial topic as speakers Baroness Caroline Cox, Prof Alister McGrath, Prof Sir John Polkinghorne, Dr Elaine Storkey and others address the theme from a Christian perspective. For more information contact Jed Atkins (jwa26) or check out the Veritas Forum website.
  • Date TBC: CGS Formal Hall
    Dress up and enjoy a scrumptious dinner in another college. Contact Nathan Mathias (jm578) for further details.
