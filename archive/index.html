Our Mission

The Christian Graduate Society (CGS) exists to make Jesus Christ known to the graduate community in Cambridge. The membership and programme reflect the high proportion of international graduates studying at the universities.

Bible Study Groups

The CGS runs five weekly Bible study groups which currently meet on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday evenings. These are graduate groups intended to be appropriate both for Christians and for those investigating the Christian faith. Invite your friends!

Please contact John Lister (lister [at] dampt.cam.ac.uk) if you would like to join one.

Fortnightly Prayer Meetings

We meet twice a month during term time for our regular prayer meetings. Please contact June Jung (sjj37 [at] cam.ac.uk) for details.

Christianity Explored Evenings

Christianity Explored Evenings won't be run this term, however, if you would like more informaiton on what is involved and when they will start again, please contact Chris Watkin (cmw36 [at] cam.ac.uk) for more information.
