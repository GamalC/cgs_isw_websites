Michaelmas 2010 Events

Sunday 10th October: CGS Fellowship Lunch

1:00pm @ I1 Nevile's Court, Trinity College ([map]). If you would like to find out more about the Christian Graduate Society, and how you can get involved by either serving and/or praying for the CGS, then please do come along! Bring your lunch. Drinks and cake provided.

Contact Matthew Kuo for further details (myhk2 [at] cam.ac.uk)

Monday 18th October: CGS Prayer Evening

TBA. An opportunity to thank God for richly blessing the International Student Welcome Events, and to pray for continued building of relationships and friendships with new grad students in Cambridge. Time to pray for CGS term events, for continued wisdom in seeking God's will in CGS activities, and the broader graduate ministry in Cambridge.

Contact Nathan Matias for further details (natematias [at] gmail.com)

Saturday 6th November: International Food Evening

7:30pm @ the Henry Martin Hall ([map]), Market St, opposite WHSmith. Come and enjoy food from around the world, and please do bring a dish from your country to share. During the evening, there will also be a short Christian talk.

Contact Ethan and Michelle Sanders for further details (es431 [at] gmail.com)

Saturday 27th November: Christmas Party

7.30pm at 236 Hills Road, the Paynes' home. Join us and the ISW crew for a 'traditional' British Christmas Party with mulled fruit punch and fantastic food. There will be a short explanation of what Christmas is and why we celebrate it. You can walk or cycle; get the Citi 1 or Citi 7 bus; or go to the University Arms hotel at 7pm for a lift. Bring yourself and your friends, especially any international friends!

Contact Naomi Hilton for further details (naomi.hilton [at] gmail.com)
