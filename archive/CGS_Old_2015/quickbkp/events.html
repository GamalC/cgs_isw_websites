Past Events

Saturday, 11th January

I hope you all had a great Christmas and a happy new year! This coming Saturday 11th we will be having an informal social before term starts up again. This will be hosted in Paula's house at 37 Panton street in south Cambridge.

Ashley Brooks will kindly set the scene playing his guitar for us, and we hope it will be a nice relaxing start to the term.

Feel free to turn up any time after 7.30pm. There is no fixed event or talks - just a chance to catch up and meet as a whole group. If you come to Connect or bible studies or even none at all you are most welcome! Some food and drinks will be provided; if you would also like to bring something to share that would be most appreciated as well.

Saturday, 7th December: Christmas party

The CGS will be having our Christmas party!!! on Saturday 7th December from 7.30pm to 10.30pm. There will be food and fun times together, with a Christian talk given by Bartow Wylie!

The party will be in the home of Professor Mike Payne at 236 Hills Road, CB2 8QE. Please do bring along friends who would also be interested!

If you are interested in coming, and unsure how to get there we'll be organising transport closer to the time, so please do email us at soc.cgs@gmail.com. (It's not too far to cycle or walk too!)

We look forward to seeing you there! Details will follow.

Michaelmas Term 2013 Events

Saturday, 9th November: Bible study training

Coming up on Saturday (9th November) we have our bible study training given by Ridley Hall vice principal and New Testament scholar Michael Thompson titled: "Interpreting Scripture and facilitating group Bible studies".

This will be great to equip us to lead bible studies and engage with the bible ourselves in private study, plus a massive opportunity to learn from a leading scholar!

We will meet in the Latimer Room in Clare college old court at 10.30am (to finish by 12) with a chance to chat together afterwards (hot drinks and biscuits provided!). I would really encourage you to come along so we can learn together how to better read and understand God's word, individually and corporately, as Christians in Cambridge.

Tuesday, 8th and Wednesday, 9th of October: Societies' Fair

For two days, the Cambridge University Students' Union runs a fair where you can find out about the student societies in Cambridge. CGS will have a stand there, so come and find us if you want to learn more about the society and how to get involved. More information here.

Sunday, 13th of October, 1.00 pm: Welcome Lunch

Come and meet other new grads and enjoy Sunday lunch in the friendly setting of 33, Victoria Park (map here).

Thursday 17th of October, 7:30 pm: Fresher's Squash

Want to find out more about CGS; what we do and how you can be involved? Then come along to the CGS Squash to meet some of the CGSers and to drink some squash! Venue: Trinity College Junior Parlour. Directions are here.

Saturday 19th of October, 10:30 pm: CGS Connect

This Saturday we've got our first Connect of term! It's at 10:30am, lasting for about an hour, in Sidney Sussex Chapel. We'll be having brunch and getting to know each other, then looking together at what the Bible has to say about the value of learning.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Easter Term 2013 Events

Sunday, 23rd of June: Garden Party

We'll be having our CGS summer garden party this Sunday 23rd June, to which you are all greatly welcomed! This year we will be in Sidney Sussex college, Tennis court gardens (Fellow's tennis court located here), which is on the corner of Jesus lane and Malcolm street. We'll meet in the gardens at 1.30pm, though we have them booked for a few hours so if you can't make it for then, please do come along when you can! There will be food and drinks (including a fruit punch!) provided, as well as a short talk from one of our members. We really look forward to seeing you there, and hope you have a great May Week!

Saturday 4th May: Film Night

This Saturday 4th May, the Christian Graduate Society will be having a Film night in Trinity college at 7pm. The film we will be watching is a dreamworks animation film: The Prince of Egypt! There will be some food and drink provided, so please do come along and you're very welcome to bring friends too! If you would like to come it would be great if you could email to confirm, so that we can prepare for numbers, but if you're unsure if you can make it, please do turn up on the night anyway - we'll have sufficient supplies and would love to see you there!

Details of the evening:
Date: Saturday 4th May
What: Movie night
Time: 7pm
Where: Trinity College, Great Court, I4 (we will be meeting outside Trinity college main gate at 7pm, so if you're not sure where the room is we can head over together)
