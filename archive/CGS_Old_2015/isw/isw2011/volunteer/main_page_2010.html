Volunteer Sign Up

If you have signed-up already, you can update your details.

If you are interested in becoming part of the ISW team, please sign up as a volunteer using the form below. As a volunteer you will work towards our mission viz. to radiate Christ's love by providing a warm welcome to the hundreds of international students, both graduates and undergraduates, who will be arriving in Cambridge this September.

We are looking for energetic servants who are interested in forming authentic friendships with arriving international students. 

Two aspects of the ISW require student volunteers:

Meeting Buses & Trains (Bus station:23 Sept - 3 Oct, 9.00 am - 9.00 pm; Train station: 27 Sept - 2 Oct, 9.00 am - 9.00 pm)
We aim to meet every bus and train coming into Cambridge from the London airports, so that we can welcome the students as they arrive. To do this, we need people who are willing to spend two hours at the bus or railway station and give students our information booklet and map, record their names if they request that we maintain contact with them, and generally be a 'friendly face.'
Visiting Students 
We follow up with internationals who have indicated interest by visiting them during their first week. Often practical help such as how to open a bank account will provide the premise for an enduring and hopefully eternal friendship.
