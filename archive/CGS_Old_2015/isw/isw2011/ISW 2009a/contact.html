Contact us

If you are keen to get in touch with us before you arrive so that we know when you are coming, or if you would like to ask us some questions about Cambridge, we would be delighted to hear from you. Fill in the following form and one of us will get back to you as soon as we can.

Please note that while we want to give you as much assistance as possible, there are unfortunately some things which we are unable to help you with. Check the areas where we can help before sending us more general questions.

For general enquiries about studying in Cambridge University, contact admissions@cam.ac.uk (for prospective undergraduates) or admissions@gradstudies.cam.ac.uk (for prospective postgraduates).
