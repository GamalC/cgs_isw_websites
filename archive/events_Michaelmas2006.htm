  • Sat 21st Oct, 2006: Trip to Ely
    Meet at Cambridge railway station at 9.40am, please bring warm clothes and a packed lunch.
    Ely is a historical town only a short trip away from Cambridge. It was founded in 637 AD as a religious community. Ely is full of centuries of English history. We will vist the magnificent cathedral and the historic 13th century house of the famous politican and revolutionary, Oliver Cromwell. The town also contains many other interesting buildings and old fashioned tea shops in which we can pleasantly spend the afternoon. Cost including entrance fees and travel will be about £8
    For more information, or to indicate you're coming, Todd Thompson tmt25@cam.ac.uk.
  • Fri 27th Oct, 2006: Formal Hall at Robinson College

    Come and enjoy formal hall together. Tickets cost about £9.
    Please contact Carrie Peffley cp318@cam.ac.uk to reserve a place.
  • Sat 4th Nov, 2006: International Food Evening
    7.30pm @ Henry Martyn Hall, Market St, opposite Borders bookshop [map for Market Street]

    Contact Anna Thompson akt29@cam.ac.uk for more details.
  • Sat 25th Nov, 2006: International Christmas Party
    7.30pm @ 236 Hills Road, between Homerton College and Addenbrooke's [map]
    Come to enjoy a festive party in a real English home. There will be a buffet supper along with a short talk about Christmas.
    About 40 mins walk/15 mins cycle from the City Centre. Alternatively take a bus: one of Citi 1 or Citi 7 (get off at Homerton College), or Babraham Road Park & Ride (usually green) (get off at Cambridge Leisure). Number 236 is on the right hand side slightly further out of town.
    Contact Xinyi Su xs204@cam.ac.uk for more details.

    See the flyer or contact Chau Ransley cdn22@cam.ac.uk for more details and travel information.
